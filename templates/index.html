About HomeShield

HomeShield is an intelligent, machine learning-powered CCTV surveillance system designed to protect the most vulnerable members of your household — the elderly and young children.

It uses YOLOv8-Pose for GPU-accelerated person detection and skeleton keypoint estimation, with a rule-based engine for fall detection and activity classification.
